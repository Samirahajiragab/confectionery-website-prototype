Coco’s Chocolatiers

Cocoa is the name associated with a powdered substance after the cacao beans are processed. Natural cocoa is dark and bitter to the taste with little to no cocoa butter. The Dutched cocoa is lighter and less bitter, but still has no cocoa butter. The cocoa butter is removed during the processing stage.
